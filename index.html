Step 2 — risk assessment
Assess the risk profile
Seven questions that determine your regulatory obligations and governance requirements.
1. What type of AI system is this?
2. Does this AI directly influence clinical decisions or patient outcomes?
3. Does it process personal health information (PHI)?
4. Is this intended for use in a regulated medical context?
5. Will it operate autonomously without human review of each output?
6. Does your organisation operate in or sell into international markets?
7. If this AI makes an error, how severe are the consequences?
8. Does this AI serve or was it trained on data from vulnerable patient populations?
