Step 2 — risk assessment
Assess the risk profile
Six questions that determine your regulatory obligations and governance requirements.
1. Does this AI directly influence decisions about individuals — clinical, financial, or otherwise?
2. Does it process personal information — health, financial, or other sensitive data?
3. What does this AI output — and who acts on it?
4. Will it operate autonomously without human review of each output?
5. Does your organisation operate in or sell into international markets?
6. Does this AI make or substantially influence decisions that directly affect individuals?
This question relates to the Privacy Act 1988 automated decision-making (ADM) transparency obligations effective 10 December 2026 — affecting all Australian organisations.
⚠ Privacy Act ADM transparency obligations likely apply. From 10 December 2026, organisations using AI to substantially influence decisions about individuals are likely to face new disclosure requirements. The OAIC is finalising guidance (consultation closed April 2026) — final scope is not yet settled. Begin preparation now and engage legal counsel to confirm your specific obligations before the deadline.
